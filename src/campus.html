ABOUT

Technival is the Annual Technical Festival of NIT , Goa
Technival provides the youth of this nation a platform to showcase their practical skills,knowledge & sets up an arena where new inventions, innovations are born.

Every year we impute ambassadors from different colleges whose main role remains with the Publicity of Technival in and around his college. This year we are to a great degree excited to expand our shoulders crosswise over various colleges of the nation.

Come become a part of TECHNIVAL'16!

RESPONSIBILITIES

PUBLICITY

Display the posters of Technival'16 on the Notice Boards of the respective college, within 3 days of receiving them

COORDINATION

Coordinating with the Technival Team in case of any events or workshops to be conducted in your college

SOCIAL MEDIA

Publicising the events of Technival on various social media including print media by sharing the links of the event
